    Lili in the tekken 8 goes around the world to fight because of her love of fighting. She has a rivalry with Asuka due to her getting beaten by her.


    Lili is a versatile character with good generics, ample plus frame pressure, and the best sidestep in the game. Her strong movement and space control make her extremely difficult to pin down, and she can punish all kinds of situations with her array of damaging whiff punish launchers and quick punishment tools.
