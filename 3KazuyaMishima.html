    Kazuya was the ceo of the Mishima Zaibatsu. He wanted to control the world by using the power of an ancient demon called azazel. But got defeated by Jin his son by using the power of an angel.


    Kazuya is a defensive and mixup-oriented Mishima-style character. He boasts the strongest, most complete punishment kit in the game, making it dangerous to do anything unsafe against him. His Mishima kit allows him to enforce a strong neutral to force an opponent's respect, and an opponent thus conditioned has to deal with his terrifying 50/50 between a safe launcher and a knockdown hellsweep that sets up the same 50/50 again.
