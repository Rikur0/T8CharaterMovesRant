    Clive Rosfield is the newest DLC character to enter Tekken 8. Before he joined Tekken 8, he was the main character in Final Fantasy 16. Clive Rosfield is a character that is a dominate that can used the power of other dominate if passed down or taken by force. His main Dominate power was Ifrit the second Eikon of Fire.


    His archetype in the game is a mixups and rushdown playstyle. He uses his phoenix dash to do starts ups or to put pressure onto the opponet. If he is put into a corner he could do a counter using Gale Claws to get out of the situation and maybe start a combo out of it.
