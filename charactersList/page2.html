Jin Kazama

Jin Kazama is a character in a game called "Tekken". He was introduced in 1997 in Tekken 3. He's the son of Kazuya Mishima and Jun Kazama.

  1. Easy for beginners to get into Tekken.
  2. Has Easy combos to learn.
  3. Has Stances that's easy to learn.

Jin Kazama can be annoying for people to fight against with his beginner stature. Players feels like he gets away to easily with his safty, chip damage and power level in the game.
