    Akuma had been seen in Tekken 7, also he is found in Street Fighter. He is the strongest fighter in the lore of tekken. He was in the game because he seeks to repay a debt to Kazumi Mishima for saving his life from an unknown critical situation.


    His playstyle in the game would be a mixups and rushdown. He could start his engage by doing Shakunetsu Messho or Tobisokuto to put on pressure or get a combo in. If he is a corner he could do Messatsu Goshoryu to get of the pressure the opponet gave to him back at him.
